Art Bacon

You might have already heard of the Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon, a game where people try to find the least amount of connections between actor Kevin Bacon and another arbitrary actor. Art Bacon is a tool that helps you find the shortest paths between any two artists.

Who connects Picasso to the Fluxus movement? How does Agnes Martin connect to Ansel Adams?

Enter the names of two artists and make some art bacon.
